Privacy Policy

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for services operated by The Seed's Minecraft Servers. This privacy notice discloses any data we may have stored on you as a player and why we store it.

By using this service, you agree to the following data being collected privately, shared with no third party:

  • All commands executed on the servers
    • This is stored and monitored to detect anomalies and potential exploits.
  • Any message via methods of communication hosted by The Seed
    • This is stored and monitored to maintain player safety and prevent predatory behavior.
  • Any in-world actions (block broken, placed, monster kill, etc...)
    • This is stored to prevent and restore malicious activity in-world.
  • Any virtual financial transaction.
    • This is stored and monitored to detect anomalies and potential exploits.
  • When you connect and disconnect from the servers
    • This data is stored to track playtime.

The following data points are collected by us and is shared with third parties:

  • All payment records, without any financial credentials.
    • This data is stored by Tebex; our store solution, as well as Paypal; our payment processor.
  • Any IP address connecting to the server.
    • This data is stored onsite as well as shared with getIPIntel for abuse prevention. getIPIntel does not record IP addresses.
